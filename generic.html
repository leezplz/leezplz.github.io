MY STORY

An aspiring multi-talented high school graduate who started everything from scratch. From being self taught to making it a career, this is her story!

As a little to no-experience graphic designer, I decided to venture into the field and built my career from there. With also volunteering in film,dance,media literacy industries on the side.

As of right now, i am confident of continuing this path through college. The list goes on as I continue to grow as an individual, but among my interests are content making, animation, technology, etc.
